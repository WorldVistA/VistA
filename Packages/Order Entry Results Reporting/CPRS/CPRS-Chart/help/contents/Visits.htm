Visits

A list of clinic visits and hospital admissions appears on the Cover Sheet.

To see the Progress Note from a clinic visit or a discharge summary from an admission, use these steps:

1.     Highlight a visit to select it.

2.     Select View | Details.

Note:      All Outpatient Encounters now have to be associated with a Visit, and include Provider, Diagnosis, and Procedure. If this information hasn't already been added (through Encounter Forms or manual entry into Scheduling, PCE, TIU, or other), you can enter it into CPRS.
