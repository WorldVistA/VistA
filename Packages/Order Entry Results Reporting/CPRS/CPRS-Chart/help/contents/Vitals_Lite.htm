Vitals Lite

Access Vitals Lite the same as before by clicking in the vitals data on the CPRS cover sheet.

 

CPRS uses the new Vitals Lite dialog to enter the following vitals and measurements:

·      Blood pressure

·      Central venous pressure

·      Circumference/girth

·      Height

·      Pain

·      Pulse

·      Pulse oximetry

·      Respiration

·      Temperature

·      Weight
