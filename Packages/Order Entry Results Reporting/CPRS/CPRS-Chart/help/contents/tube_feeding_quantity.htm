Tubefeeding Quantity

The following may be entered for quantity:

·     Units: K for Kcals; M for ml; O for oz.; or U for units (such as cans); PKG

·     Frequency: Daily, Hour, QH, BID, TID, QID, Q2H, Q3H, Q4H, or Q6H

 

Or 100 ml/HR X 16 for 16 hours

 

·     If powder form product, then (# GRAMS or # Unit or PKG)/FREQUENCY

 

Examples:

20 GRAMS/DAY

1 PKG/TID

6 U/D

1 U/Q3H

50ml/TID, 100 ML/HR
