What's New in CPRS GUI v.25 (OR*3.0*195)

Clinical Indicators Data Capture (CIDC)

·      Entering Diagnosis Codes for Specific Orders (CIDC Overview)-This topic contains information about what was added for this project, including what kinds of orders it affects, what developers have added, and what feature have been included to make this easier for providers.

 

·      Changes on the Review / Sign Changes dialog-Changes were made here to document the new features for adding diagnosis information to orders.

 

·      Changes on the Sign Selected dialog-Developers also made changes to the Sign Selected dialog to help providers enter diagnosis information.

 

·      Creating and Maintaining a Personal Diagnosis List-Developers added a way for providers to create a personal list of often-used diagnoses to help them more efficiently enter these for the required orders.

 

·      Understanding Service Connection and Treatment Factors- This information helps explain what makes a patient service connected and what the various treatment factors mean.

 

·      Entering Allergies: New information about entering allergies via the Cover Sheet tab and changes to previous information about viewing and entering allergies via the Orders tab.

 

·      Postings: Updated information about postings (including information about viewing and creating new postings).

 

·      Inpatient Medications for Outpatients (IMO): Added screen captures to illustrate the two new display groups for IMO medications.

 

·      New Duration/Total Volume field. Added a new screen capture and instructions for the new Duration/Total Volume field on the IV Fluids dialog.
