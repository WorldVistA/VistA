Customizing CPRS

You can customize CPRS in the following ways:

·      Change font size

To change the font size, select Edit | Preferences, then choose a different font size.

·      Change the date ranges for items that appear on the Cover Sheet

·      Change your patient list

·      Add Quick Orders or Order Sets

·      Change your notifications and the way they behave

·      Add or delete your order checks

·      Change printing formats and other parameters (Check with your Clinical Coordinator or IRM specialist.)

·      Make other programs (e.g., non-VA applications such as Excel) accessible through the Tools menu
