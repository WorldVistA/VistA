Help Menu

The Help menu includes:

· Contents

· Last Broker Call

· Show List Box Data

· Symbol Table

· About

Note:      Last Broker Call, Show List Box Data, and Symbol Table are troubleshooting menu items for IRMs. If you are not IRM staff, you will not have these on your menu.

 

Contents

Whenever you need more information about anything in CPRS, click on Help on the menu bar at the top of the Window, and choose Contents. After the Contents window opens, you can pick one of the items listed, go to an index, or enter a subject to search for.
