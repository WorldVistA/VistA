Exit

When you select File | Exit, if you have no unsigned orders or notes, the patient chart closes and you exit completely from CPRS.

If you have unsigned orders or notes, the Review/Sign Changes dialog box appears. Your ability to determine whether orders should be saved with a signature, do not require a signature, or can be saved without a signature (by checking the options at the bottom of the dialog) depends on the key you have been assigned and the kind of order.
