Signing a Surgery Report

Depending on the configuration of your site and your access permissions, you may be able to sign certain surgery reports.

To sign a surgery report, follow these steps:

1.       Select the Surgery tab.

2.     Select a surgery report from the All Surgery Cases pane.

3.     Select Action | Sign Report Now...

-or-

right-click in the details pane and select Sign Report Now .

 

4.     Enter your electronic signature code.

5.       Select Sign .
