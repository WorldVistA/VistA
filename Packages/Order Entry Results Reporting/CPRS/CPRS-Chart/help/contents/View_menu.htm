View Menu

The View menu changes depending on which tab you are on. It enables you to move between the different tabs and to change your view of the information on a tab or obtain more information. For example, when you are on the Problems tab, the View menu presents you with the options of viewing different categories of problems such as viewing only active problems or using filters to define locations, a provider, and whether you want inpatient, outpatient, or both.
