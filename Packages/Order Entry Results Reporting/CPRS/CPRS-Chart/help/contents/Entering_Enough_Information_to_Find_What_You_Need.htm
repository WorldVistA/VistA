Entering Enough Information to Find What You Need

Choosing the right words or numbers to search for will find the right template more quickly.

For example, if you wanted to find a template called "patient's arthritis," and you have several templates with both "arthritis" and "patient's" in the title, you will need to enter both words in the right order.

But, if you only have one template with the word "glaucoma," you might have to enter only "gla" to find it.

Choose your search words carefully and you will get what you want faster.
