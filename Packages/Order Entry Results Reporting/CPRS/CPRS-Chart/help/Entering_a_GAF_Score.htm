Entering a GAF Score

To enter a GAF score, your location must be a mental health clinic. In addition, you must be creating or editing an unsigned progress note and be in the encounter form.

 

To enter a GAF score, use these steps:

  1.  If you are in the encounter frame, skip to step 3. Otherwise, select the Notes tab.

  2. Select New Note and enter the note title, date, author, and other information.

  3. Select Encounter.

  4. Select the GAF tab.

  5. Enter the score, who determined the score, and the date.

  6. Select OK.
