GAF Score (overview)

You can enter a Global Assessment of Functioning (GAF) score through CPRS. To enter a GAF score, two things are required:

·      You must have the Mental Health package and two specific Mental Health patches installed.

·      Your location must be a mental health clinic.

The GAF tab is on the Encounter frame so you must be creating or editing a progress note to enter a GAF score.

The GAF tab has

·      A display of as many as 5 previous scores, if available, including the score, date determined, the name of the person that determined the score, and a comment, which is used only if a score was entered in error.

·      Places to enter a new score, date, and person who determined the score

·      A Reference Information button that launches your web browser to bring up a web page that describes the GAF score.
