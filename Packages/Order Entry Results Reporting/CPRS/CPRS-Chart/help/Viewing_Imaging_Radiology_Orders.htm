Viewing Imaging/Radiology Orders

To see a list of pending or completed Imaging exams, follow these steps:

1.     Select the Orders tab.

The orders for the selected patient are displayed. The orders are in order by service. The service is shown in the first column of the list of orders.

 

2.     Locate the imaging orders, scrolling down the list of orders if necessary.

3.     If necessary, change the category of the orders displayed:

a.    Select the View menu.

b.    Select the desired category

o     Active Orders

o     Expiring Orders

o     Unsigned Orders

o     Custom Order View...
